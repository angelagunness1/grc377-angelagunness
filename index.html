Hello, we are Bauhaus Coffee & Gallery.
Bauhaus is a locally owned Gallery & Coffee Shop.

Bauhaus is looking to revolutionize the way people experience their daily coffee ritual with combining coffee with the enjoyment of local artist work displayed in their gallery. Bauhaus serves up top-quality coffee is our relentless standard, and believes people are at the heart of our business.

Bauhaus is located at the heart of Encinitas, California on the 101 hwy. Bauhaus is a small, local business looking to serve the people of San Diego with their freshly roasted coffee, fine imported teas and freshly baked goods. At the same time Bauhaus is also looking to inspire their customers by supporting and displaying local artists in their gallery each month.
